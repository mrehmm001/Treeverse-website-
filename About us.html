ABOUT US

We are an organisation who cares for our environment. We only have one Earth, so we better make sure we protect it and preserve the ecosystem, wildlife and the environment! We have bases all across the world, from all the way east of Japan to west of California, but we are still expanding. Our purpose is to plant trees. Trees server many purposes in our daily lives, they produce the air we breathe! But most importantly, take in carbon emissions, making the air clear from any pollutants. We have about 50,000 volunteers working with us, and have recently achieved an astonishing goal of planting 3 million trees within the first 2 months of our operation!

THE PROCESS
THE PROCESS

The process goes like this, when someone donates on our official website, we receive their donation. We forward our donations to environmentalist foundation institute where we collect our tree saplings. 10% of our total donations goes towards wildlife protection institute, where their job is to protect the wildlife. From there, we use your nations to plant a tree wherever you wished to be planted, for instance, if you wanted your tree to be planted in Asia, our volunteers in Asia will plant your trees there.

WHY WE PLANT TREES?

Why do we plant trees? First thing first, trees provide the air we breathe and filter air pollution. Trees also takes in carbon which helps in reducing carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gasses from the atmosphere. Trees also help reduce the ozone levels in urban areas. Trees properly placed around buildings can reduce air conditioning needs by 30%! Over the course its life, a single tree can absorb one ton of carbon dioxide, we need trees to live, trees need us for it to live.
